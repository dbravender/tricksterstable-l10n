Game Overview

You and your partner (who sits across from you) want to capture 4 or more Boss Yokai (7s) to win a round. You will lose the round if you win 7 tricks before capturing 4 Boss Yokai. If all tricks are played, then the team who won the last trick wins the round.

Card Distributions

Round Setup

The deck of 49 cards is shuffled. 12 cards are dealt face-down to each player.

Next, one card is dealt face-up which determines the Trump Suit for the round.

Before the Round Begins

Each player selects three cards to pass to their partner. You may pass any cards. In the Trickster's Table implementation your partner is expecting you to short suit yourself in blue and they are attempting to short suit themselves in red.

In the first round of a game the player with the green A leads. The lead moves clockwise to the next player after each hand.

Game Play Rules

Each round, you'll play multiple turns called Tricks where each player will play one card from their hand. The Lead Player will play first. They choose any card from their hand and play it face-up in front of them. In clockwise order, the other players must now play a card of the same suit as the Lead Player. If a player has no cards of that suit, they may play any card in their hand.

Taking a trick

After all players have played a card, check to see if anyone played the A card. If they did, that player takes the trick.

If no one played the A card, check if anyone played a card of the Trump Suit. If anyone did, then the highest card of that suit takes the trick.

Otherwise, if all players played the same suit then the highest card takes the trick. If two different suits were played then the highest card of the suit of the card played by the Lead Player takes the trick.

The player who took the trick now becomes the Lead Player.

End of a Round

The round is over, and a round winner is determined, if any of the following has happened.

  • A team that has captured a total of 4 or more Boss Yokai cards wins the round.
  • A team has taken 7 tricks but has captured 3 or fewer Boss Yokai cards. The other team wins the round.
  • No one has cards left in their hands. The team who won the last trick wins the round.

Scoring

Only the team who won a round scores points. The other team does not score, regardless of how many cards they took.

If a team has won the round because the other team took 7 tricks, the winning team takes all Boss Yokai cards remaining in all players' hands. They add these to the Boss Yokai they took this round.

The winning team scores as many points as there are white stars on Boss Yokai they captured this round (ignoring the Boss Yokai of the trump suit).

End of the Game

If a team has 7 or more points they win!

Otherwise, a new hand is dealt and another round is played. The lead alternates between players.
