Objective

In Pala: Impressionism, you do not want to score points. The game ends when a player reaches 45 points or more; the player with the lowest score wins.

Deck Composition

48 cards (8 each of red, blue, and yellow - 1,1,2,2,3,3,4,5; 8 each of orange, green, and purple - 2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9)

Setup

  1. Each player is dealt 11 cards.
  2. The remaining 4 cards are discarded face-down and are not used for the current hand.

Bidding

In turn order, each player may play one card onto an available slot or pass; you cannot bid a color already played. Continue until all four slots are filled. The colors on the board determine scoring at round's end; card values are irrelevant.

Trick Play

  1. Lead: Winner of the previous trick plays any one card to set the trick's initial color.
  2. Follow Suit: In clockwise order, if you have a card in the trick's color:
    • Primary (red/blue/yellow): play one card of that color.
    • Secondary (orange/green/purple): either play one card of that color or mix.
    If you have no cards in the trick's color, you may:
    • Mix (secondary only): play two primary cards that combine to the secondary color; their values sum.
    • Smear (primary only): play one different primary card onto the current winning card to change the color, then play one secondary card of that new color.
    • Junk: play any single non-matching card.

Awarding Tricks

After all have played, the highest-value card (or summed value if mixed) in the trick's final color wins; the winner takes all cards into their score pile and leads the next trick.

Ending the Round

Players who run out of cards sit out. The round ends after a trick if the winner has no cards or only one player remains with cards.

Scoring

  • Take all won-trick cards into your score pile, then add any remaining hand cards.
  • For each card in your pile:
    • Color not on board: discard it (0 points).
    • X space (cancel) color: discard one other card for each (highest value cards will automatically be discarded); excess cancel cards are -1 point each.
    • +1 space colors: 1 point per card.
    • +# space color: score the card's face value in points.

End of Game

If no one has reached 45 points, the cards are all shuffled and another hand is played. The game ends when a player reaches 45 or more points; the player with the lowest score wins.
